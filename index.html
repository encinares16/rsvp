How It All Began

Our Story

✦

"Some love stories are slow burns, written in coffee shops and quiet weekdays — and some change your life in a single afternoon."

2019 · The Beginning

Rose and Rome met at a mutual friend's gallery opening in Manila. Rome spotted Rose across the room, convinced she was the most interesting person there — he was absolutely right. They talked until the gallery closed, and then for three more hours on the steps outside.

2021 · Adventures & Big Moves

After two years of weekday trips to the coast, cooking disasters in their tiny apartment, and a memorable road trip through the Pacific Northwest, Rome surprised Rose with a proposal under the stars at their favorite vineyard in Fairmount Hill Event Place, Antipolo Rizal — the very place they are now getting married.

2026 · Forever

Now, surrounded by their dearest family and friends in the place that started it all, Rose and Rome are ready to begin the most beautiful chapter of their story. We are so grateful you are here to celebrate it with us.
